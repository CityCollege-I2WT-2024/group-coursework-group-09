The 1970s brought significant changes to society, marking a period of freedom and creativity. This had a strong influence on fashion, as people began to reject strict dress codes and use their clothing as a way to express their personalities, beliefs, and values.

The 1970s did not bring just one style but a variety of them, with the most iconic being: hippie fashion, disco glamour, and the bold aesthetics of glam rock.
