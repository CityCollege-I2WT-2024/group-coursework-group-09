1940s Wartime and Utility Fashion

Fashion in the 1940s was heavily influenced by World War II, resulting in practical, resource-conscious designs that emphasized utility while maintaining style.Accessories were minimal, and muted or neutral colors like navy, brown, and khaki dominated, though bright floral prints and playful patterns offered moments of cheer amidst wartime austerity.
