Fashion Icons: 2010s and 2020s

Kendall Jenner-2010

Kendall Jenner, born on May 3, 1995, in Los Angeles, California, is part of the famous Kardashian-Jenner family. She is the daughter of Kris Jenner and Olympic gold medalist Caitlyn Jenner, and the half-sister of Kim Kardashian. Kendall first gained attention through the reality TV series Keeping Up with the Kardashians, which chronicles the lives of her family. However, she quickly distinguished herself from her siblings with her successful career in modeling. Growing up in the spotlight, Jenner’s early years were marked by a mix of public exposure and a desire to pursue a career outside of the family’s entertainment ventures. She became a highly sought-after model and has since built a strong personal brand, becoming one of the most successful models of her generation.

Kendall Jenner is considered one of the leading fashion icons of the 21st century. Her modeling career took off in the early 2010s when she signed with the prestigious modeling agency Wilhelmina Models and soon became a prominent figure on runways and in major ad campaigns. Known for her tall, slender frame and striking beauty, Jenner has walked for top designers like Chanel, Versace, Givenchy, and Balmain, and appeared on the covers of magazines such as Vogue and Harper’s Bazaar. Her personal style is often described as a mix of casual streetwear with high-fashion elements, and she has become a muse for many fashion houses. Jenner’s influence extends beyond modeling; her chic, off-duty looks and collaborations with brands such as Adidas and Calvin Klein further cemented her as a fashion trendsetter.

Timothée Chalamet-2020

Timothée Chalamet, born on December 27, 1995, in New York City, is an American actor who quickly rose to prominence with his remarkable talent and distinctive style. Raised in a creative environment, Chalamet’s father is an editor and his mother is a former Broadway dancer. He studied acting at the LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts before attending the Gallatin School of Individualized Study at NYU. Timothée first garnered major attention with his breakthrough role in Call Me by Your Name (2017), for which he received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor. His subsequent roles in films such as Lady Bird (2017), Little Women (2019), and Dune (2021) solidified his position as one of Hollywood’s most versatile and sought-after actors. Known for his charm, depth, and unique on-screen presence, Chalamet has become a rising star in the film industry.

Timothée Chalamet has become a fashion icon known for his fearless and unconventional style. From his early appearances on the red carpet to his more recent roles, he has consistently pushed boundaries with his clothing choices, often opting for high-fashion, gender-fluid looks. His bold approach to fashion includes embracing both classic tailoring and more avant-garde, edgy designs, frequently opting for pieces from designers like Haider Ackermann, Louis Vuitton, and Alexander McQueen. Chalamet’s style is a refreshing departure from traditional Hollywood menswear, mixing modernity with a touch of old-school charm. His outfits, which often feature unexpected colors, textures, and silhouettes, have earned him recognition as one of the best-dressed men in the industry. Chalamet’s fashion sensibility is celebrated for its sophistication, playfulness, and inclusivity, influencing both the fashion world and pop culture.
