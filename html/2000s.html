Fashion in 2000s

The 2000s continued the experimental spirit of the late 90s while incorporating futuristic elements and embracing the rise of technology. It was a decade defined by contrasts—minimalism coexisted with bold maximalist trends, and casual streetwear blended effortlessly with glamorous red-carpet styles. The fashion of the 2000s reflected the rapid evolution of pop culture, technology, and global influences.
